Our Story

Born from a Passion for the World's Finest Chocolate

The Chocolate Room was born in 2006 with a simple dream — to bring the authentic taste of Belgian chocolate to every Indian city. What started as a single café has grown into India's largest premium chocolate café chain.

We source only Callebaut Belgian couverture chocolate — the same chocolate used by master chocolatiers in Brussels. Every cup, every dessert, every truffle is crafted fresh, every single day.
